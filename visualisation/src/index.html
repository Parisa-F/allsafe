Do you know what are the top 5 scams in number of reports?

Use the drop-down menu to switch between 2024 and an overview from 2020 to 2024.

The top 2 common social media scams in 2024 are buying or selling and gain personal information scams.

Buying or selling scams are the most common, making up over 44% from 2020 till now, and over 427 reports within 2024.

Under buying or selling scams, online shopping scams being the largest type that took around 41% and having 1,392 reports over the period.

Gain personal information scams are the second-largest, about 22% with 1,673 reports during the whole period.

Does the money lost follow a similar pattern as the number of reports?

While the top 5 most frequent scams align with the top 5 financial loss scams, the patterns have some differences.

In 2024, the top 5 scams alone accounted for approximately $3.27 million in losses, representing more than 90% of the total amount lost on social media scams with young adults.

Among these years, investment scams have the highest financial impact, making up 50% of the total losses. Dating and romance scams account for around 17%.

No matter which type of scam you are facing, the potential loss can accumulate into a vast amount and continuously influence our daily lives.
